Our story

Built on quality, honesty and hard work.

Founded in 1994, Electro Power Engineering has built a reputation for quality engineering, precision workmanship and honest, reliable service.

Following founder Peter’s passing in 2024, Jonny and Ronnie took responsibility for carrying the company forward. With 23 and 14 years at the company respectively, they continue the standards and personal service Peter established.

Honouring a proud engineering legacy since 1994.
Read our full story +

Our founder, Peter, wasn’t just the owner of the business—he was a dear friend, mentor and someone who was deeply respected throughout the engineering industry. We were more like a family than colleagues, and this company was his life’s work.

Peter believed in doing every job properly, treating customers fairly and charging an honest price, even when it wasn’t always the most profitable decision. His reputation was built on integrity, skill and genuinely caring about the people he worked with. Those values continue to guide us every day.

Following Peter’s passing in 2024, we were determined to ensure that the business he dedicated his life to would continue. From August 2024, we took responsibility for running the company, maintaining the same standards and personal service our customers had always received.

Between us, Jonny has dedicated 23 years to the company and Ronnie 14 years. We didn’t buy a business we barely knew—we took over the business we’d helped build. We learned our trade under Peter’s guidance, and every job we complete reflects the pride, care and attention to detail that he expected. More than anything, we want to honour his legacy by continuing the business he loved and making him proud.

Looking to the future is just as important as preserving the past. That’s why we’re proud to have welcomed Jay as our apprentice. Passing on skills, knowledge and a strong work ethic is something Peter believed in, and Jay has already proved himself to be hardworking, eager to learn and always willing to muck in.

Today, we’re proud to carry the company forward with the same commitment to quality, fairness and customer service that has defined the business since 1994. Whether you’ve been with us for many years or are contacting us for the first time, you’ll receive the same honest advice, skilled workmanship and personal service that Peter built his reputation on.

Peter built this company on quality, honesty and hard work. Our promise is simple: to honour that legacy every day by continuing to deliver the service our customers have trusted since 1994.
